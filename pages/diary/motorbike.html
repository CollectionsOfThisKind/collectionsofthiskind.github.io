Motorbike

I picked up my daughter from the head of the valley, and after a few miles in the warm setting sun I asked her, "shall we take the short way or the long way home?" And so we went the long way home, with the sun gold on our sides and all the world ablaze.

One day, I shall die, but this trip will live on forever in space and time and my love for my girl shall be known.

28 Aug 2021
