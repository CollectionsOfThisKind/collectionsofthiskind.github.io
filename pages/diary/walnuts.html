Walnuts

I am reminded of those other great nuts in erotisism: Johann Burchard's Banquet of Chestnuts.

On opening page 129 of Eduard Fuchs' Geschichte Der Erotischen Kunst Das Individuelle Problem, II., I found a small piece of walnut membrane. I rather like the idea of some 1930s German cracking walnuts while perusing their new book of erotic prints.
