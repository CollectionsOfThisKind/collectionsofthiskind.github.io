Blood on blood on blood

True horror.

Waking upon that supranatural instinct that something is wrong, and going downstairs to the kitchen to find my child, hunched on the floor, crimson rivulets from every surface of arms, legs, face, torso, crying with the terror of what they've wrought on themselves.

What is there to be done, but hold them close, love them, and pick your way through the wounds.

06 Sept 2023
