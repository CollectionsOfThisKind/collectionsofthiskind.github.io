The Threat of Artificial Intelligence

It seems a popular position, faced with the uncanniness of modern machine learning, to regard AI as a threat to the human race that needs stringent control. But we happily ignore that AI is simply a meta-intelligence1, founded on all humanity has produced that can be expressed. It isn't AI that we need to improve, but human interactions. AI simply refects its inputs.2

 

Notes:

1 The potential for new knowledge to emerge from the mass is considerable, whereas I suspect the threats are old fashioned.

2 I speak here as someone who has worked as a researcher building AIs and Artificial Life for the last 20-odd years.
