Reboot books1

Favoured teen reading.

If anyone in the future wants to re-create me, this is where I'd start.
  • Conrad, Joseph (1899) Heart of Darkness
  • Hesse, Hermann (1931) Das Glasperlenspiel
  • Sartre, Jean Paul (1938) La Nausée
  • Camus, Albert (1942) L'Étranger
  • Persig, Robert (1974) Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance
  • Aitken, Robert (1982) Taking the Path of Zen

 

Notes:

1In 2018 I had a six month breakdown that oscillated between days of screaming into the void and exhausted unconsciousness; after six months of living for the next breath I was a total void. I decided to rebuild from the ground up, and reread a lot of the books I read as a teenager, filtering out the unhelpful. Those above were my starting point, and thank the gods of literature for that.
