The Colour of Monkery

Monks:1

  • Benedictine ("Black Monks"): black
    • Cluniac: black (now extinct)
    • Olivetan Benedictines: white
    • Cistercians ("White Monks"): Undyed light brown
      (but often called white) with black cover
      • Trappists: white/grey under black
  • Carthusians: white

 

Canons:1

  • Augustinians: white with black cover
  • Premonstratensians: white

 

Hermits:1

  • Camaldolese: white
  • Carmelites: brown with white cover (now friars)
  • Carthusians: white (now monks)

 

Friars:1

  • Carmelites ("Whitefriars"): brown with white cloak
  • Franciscans ("Grayfriars"): undyed brown
    • Capuchins: cappuccino brown (literally)
    • Franciscan Friars of the Immaculate: blue
  • Dominicans ("Blackfriars"): white with black cloak
  • Augustinians: black, but in hot countries white
  • Trinitarians: white with a blue and red cross.

 

Bonus Nuns:

  • Sisters of finding Jesus in the Temple ("Blue Nuns"): blue
  • Celestine / Turchine ("Blue Nuns"): blue
  • The Children of Mary: purple
  • Augustinian Recollect Nazarene Nuns : purple
  • Redemptoristines: red
  • Holy Spirit Adoration Sisters ("Pink Sisters"): pink
