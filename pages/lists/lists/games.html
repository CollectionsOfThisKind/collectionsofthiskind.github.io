Fun for Boys and Girls

A growing checklist of obscure games.

A selection of unusual games (sources in notes unless I've played them). Caveat Lector: while the authors treat the names as familiar or real, some could easily be fictional.

 

Games, imaginary or otherwise
  • Wallops: skittles with stick not ball1
  • Cat-at-the-Window2
  • Goblin of the Well3
  • 'Fools' and 'Millers'4
  • Mop-and-Heede: hide and seek5
  • Slaps: fast reaction hand slapping game
  • Dog and Duck: goal-based ball game played on a long rectangular field6
  • Hunt the Slipper7
  • Kiss in the Ring7
  • Cross your Toes7
  • Last Man Out7
  • Chase the Cherry7
  • Count your Blessings7
  • Mumblety-peg: knife retrieval game8
  • Chop-cherry: applebobbing, but with a suspended cherry9
  • The Gaping, wide-mouthed, waddling frog: question and forfeit game10
  • Light as a Feather, Stiff as a Board: person lifting game11
  • Dandy Shandy: ball dodging game12
  • Huckle Buckle Beanstalk: hunt the thimble game13
  • High Cockalorum / Polly on the Mopstick / Hunch, Cuddy, Hunch: guessing under stress game14
