Gimcracks

A growing list of things that everyone assumes have uses, but apparently don't:1

  • The indents ('punts') at the bottom of wine bottles.
  • The paper on the top of butter and marge in cartons.
  • Washing-up bowls.2

Notes:

1 And here I mean, myths and speculation aside, current manufacturers of these objects have been widely consulted and don't know, other than people want to see them.

2 Possibly of use when sinks were made of heavy porcelain and things broke against them, but the overlap between porcelain sinks and plastic tubs seems limited.
